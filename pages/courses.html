Artificial Intelligence

Description:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly computer systems. These processes include learning , reasoning, and self-correction. AI enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions. Course Overview:
