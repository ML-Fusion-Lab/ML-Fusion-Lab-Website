3. Feature Extraction Using Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCCs)


    # Extract MFCCs from audio data
    mfccs = librosa.feature.mfcc(y=audio_data, sr=sr, n_mfcc=13)
    plt.figure(figsize=(10, 4))
    librosa.display.specshow(mfccs, sr=sr, x_axis='time')
    plt.colorbar()
    plt.title('MFCCs of Urban Sound')
    plt.xlabel('Time (seconds)')
    plt.ylabel('MFCC Coefficients')
    plt.show()

Extract MFCCs, a widely used feature in audio processing, to represent the sound characteristics. This helps in distinguishing different urban sounds for classification.
